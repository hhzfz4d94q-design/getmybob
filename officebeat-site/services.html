Services

Six practices, one operating model. Each starts with a 2-week diagnostic and ends with your team — not us — owning the steady state.

01 · Enterprise risk management programs

Stand up or modernize ERM frameworks — risk taxonomy, appetite statements, governance forums, key risk indicators, and board-ready reporting — aligned to COSO ERM, ISO 31000, and your sector's expectations.

  • Risk taxonomy and appetite
  • Tier-1 risk register
  • Governance charter & cadence
  • KRI / KPI dashboard design
  • Board & audit-committee reporting pack
  • Year-1 implementation roadmap

02 · Compliance & controls

Translate regulatory complexity — SOX, HIPAA, GDPR, FFIEC, NYDFS, state privacy laws — into pragmatic, testable controls and the evidence that proves them. We build control libraries you can actually audit against.

  • Regulatory inventory + applicability
  • Control library & design assessment
  • Testing scripts & sample evidence
  • Issue tracking + remediation plans
  • Internal audit / SOX-ITGC support
  • Regulator response packs

03 · Cyber & technology risk

NIST CSF, ISO 27001, third-party risk, and incident readiness — assessed against your operating reality, prioritized by exposure, and built into how your engineering and IT teams already work.

  • NIST CSF / ISO 27001 maturity assessment
  • Critical-asset and crown-jewel mapping
  • Third-party / SaaS risk inventory
  • Incident response playbooks & tabletop
  • Cloud (AWS / Azure / GCP) control baseline
  • Cyber insurance readiness review

04 · Digital & AI transformation

Risk-aware modernization — cloud migration, data platform rebuilds, AI adoption — so innovation does not outpace governance and governance does not strangle innovation. Built for healthcare, fintech, and other regulated industries.

  • Transformation risk register
  • AI governance & model inventory
  • Data classification & protection rules
  • Vendor selection due diligence
  • Migration cutover risk plans
  • Post-migration audit-readiness review

05 · Vendor & third-party risk

Right-size third-party risk programs so the right vendors get fast lanes and the risky ones get the scrutiny. Pre-onboarding due diligence, ongoing monitoring, and renewal-time reassessment without bottlenecking the business.

  • Tiering & criticality framework
  • Standardized due-diligence questionnaire
  • Ongoing monitoring playbook
  • Renewal & offboarding checklists
  • SOC 2 / ISO review pipeline
  • Vendor risk reporting dashboard

06 · Fractional risk advisory

Plug-in risk leadership for moments that do not justify a full-time hire — audits, board prep, M&A integration, pre-funding due diligence, or covering a leadership transition. Engaged by the month, not the hour.

  • Interim CRO / risk lead
  • Board / audit-committee prep
  • M&A risk diligence (pre and post)
  • Pre-funding / investor diligence
  • Crisis & incident command
  • Hand-off & recruiting your full-time hire
